General Overview of Sustainability and how to it achieve it in electricity

Sustainability is a perspective that incorporates living well now and in the future. It is the adoption of policies that aid in the pursuit of equity through time for resources that are natural, economic and social.

Sustainable electricity is the conversion to an all renewable resources power generation. At present electricity generation is a principle global warming agent as it causes about 26% of greenhouse gas emissions. This requires a change of action in how the world acquires its electric power. A rerouting to predominantly renewable solar and wind energy resources can decrease the burden of rising carbon dioxide emissions. While these approaches may be difficult to implement on large scales, continuing research and effective application prove that not only will solar and wind help in the preservation of environment but also result in economic efficiency through reduced costs and social development through upgrading technology.
